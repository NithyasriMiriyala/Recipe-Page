Wonton Soup

Ingredients for Wontons:

  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/4 lb raw shrimp, peeled, deveined, and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Wonton wrappers

Ingredients for Soup:

  • 6 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, chopped shrimp, minced garlic, chopped green onion, soy sauce, sesame oil, minced ginger, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine.
  • To assemble the wontons, place a small spoonful of the filling mixture in the center of each wonton wrapper. Moisten the edges with water, then fold the wrapper over the filling to form a triangle. Press the edges firmly to seal, making sure there are no air pockets.
  • In a large pot, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a simmer over medium heat. Add the minced garlic, sliced ginger, and chopped green onions.
  • Carefully drop the wontons into the simmering broth and cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the wontons are cooked through and float to the surface.
  • Stir in the soy sauce and sesame oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve the wonton soup hot, garnished with additional chopped green onions if desired.
  • Enjoy your delicious homemade Wonton Soup!
