Vietnamese Sizzling Cake (Banh Xeo) Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced onions
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced pork belly
  • 1/2 cup shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup nuoc cham sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the rice flour, cornstarch, turmeric, and salt. Gradually whisk in the water and coconut milk until smooth.
  2. Stir in the green onions and set aside.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a 10-inch non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onions and cook until softened, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the pork belly and cook until browned, about 3 minutes.
  5. Add the shrimp and cook until pink, about 2 minutes.
  6. Add the bean sprouts and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Pour the batter over the filling in the skillet, spreading it evenly. Cover and cook for 5 minutes, or until the edges are crispy and the center is cooked through.
  8. Slide the cake onto a plate and top with the basil, cilantro, and mint leaves.
  9. Serve with nuoc cham sauce for dipping.
