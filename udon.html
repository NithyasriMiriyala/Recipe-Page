Udon Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 packs (about 7 oz each) udon noodles
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms (shiitake or cremini)
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: sliced boiled egg, nori seaweed, tofu cubes

Instructions

  • In a large pot, combine the vegetable or chicken broth and water. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  • Add the sliced mushrooms, carrots, and garlic to the pot. Simmer for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  • Stir in the soy sauce, mirin, and sesame oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Add the udon noodles to the pot and cook according to package instructions until they are heated through and tender.
  • Stir in the baby spinach leaves and sliced green onions. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the spinach is wilted.
  • Remove the pot from the heat. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  • Divide the udon noodle soup into bowls and serve hot.
  • Garnish with optional toppings such as sliced boiled egg, nori seaweed, or tofu cubes, if desired.
