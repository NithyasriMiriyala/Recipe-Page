Tom Yum Goong

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken or seafood broth
  • 200g shrimp (prawns), peeled and deveined
  • 200g mushrooms (straw mushrooms or button mushrooms), sliced
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, cut into 2-inch pieces and bruised
  • 3-4 kaffir lime leaves, torn
  • 2-3 red bird's eye chilies, sliced (adjust to taste)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-inch piece galangal or ginger, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves, chopped

Instructions

  • In a pot, bring the chicken or seafood broth to a boil over medium heat.
  • Add the lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, red chilies, garlic, and galangal or ginger to the boiling broth. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes to infuse the flavors.
  • Add the sliced mushrooms to the pot and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  • Add the peeled shrimp (prawns) to the pot and cook until they turn pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes.
  • Stir in the fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  • Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the chopped cilantro leaves.
  • Serve the Tom Yum Goong hot in bowls, garnished with additional cilantro leaves if desired.
  • Enjoy your delicious homemade Tom Yum Goong!
