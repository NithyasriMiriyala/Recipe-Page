Ramen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • 1 tablespoon sake
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 packages ramen noodles
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup sliced bok choy
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • Toasted sesame oil, for drizzling
  • Sriracha, for drizzling (optional)

    • 1Heat the oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Season the pork with ¼ teaspoon each salt and pepper and cook until cooked through, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Let rest for 5 minutes before thinly slicing.  1. Add the scallion whites and ginger to the drippings in the Dutch oven. Cook, stirring, until softened, 1 to 2 minutes. Add the broth and bring it to a boil. Add the noodles and boil, stirring occasionally, until tender, 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in the soy sauce.  2. Serve the soup topped with pork, carrot, radishes, cilantro, and scallion greens.
