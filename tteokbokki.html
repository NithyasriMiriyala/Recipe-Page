Tteokbokki

Ingredients

  • 200g tteok (Korean rice cakes), soaked in water for 30 minutes
  • 2 cups water or anchovy broth
  • 1/4 cup gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • 1 tbsp gochugaru (Korean red pepper flakes)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • Optional toppings: boiled eggs, fish cakes, sliced cheese

Instructions

  • In a pot, combine water (or anchovy broth), gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, and sugar. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring until the ingredients are well combined.
  • Add the sliced onion and minced garlic to the boiling sauce. Let it simmer for 5 minutes to infuse the flavors.
  • Add the soaked rice cakes and sliced cabbage to the pot. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the rice cakes are soft and the sauce has thickened.
  • Stir in the green onions and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  • Serve the tteokbokki hot, garnished with optional toppings like boiled eggs, fish cakes, or sliced cheese.
  • Enjoy your delicious homemade tteokbokki!
