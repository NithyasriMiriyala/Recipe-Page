Cơm Tấm Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked sticky rice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup grilled pork belly, diced
  • 1/4 cup pickled vegetables (carrots, daikon radish)
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • 1/4 cup crispy fried shallots
  • Fish sauce caramel (for drizzling)
  • Fresh cilantro leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a round cake pan with butter or cooking spray.
  • In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Heat over medium heat until the sugar has dissolved.
  • Add the cooked sticky rice to the saucepan and stir until well combined. Let it cook for a few minutes until the rice absorbs the coconut milk mixture and becomes sticky.
  • Transfer the sticky rice mixture to the greased cake pan, pressing it down firmly to form an even layer. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes until the top is slightly golden.
  • Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool slightly. Once cooled, unmold the cake onto a serving platter.
  • Top the cake with diced grilled pork belly, pickled vegetables, crushed peanuts, and crispy fried shallots.
  • Drizzle fish sauce caramel over the cake for a savory-sweet flavor contrast.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves before serving.
  • Slice and enjoy your unique and delicious Cơm Tấm Cake!
