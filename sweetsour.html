Sweet and Sour Pork

Ingredients for the Pork:

  • 1 lb pork loin or tenderloin, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Ingredients for the Sweet and Sour Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (for thickening)

Instructions

  • Season the pork pieces with salt and pepper.
  • In a shallow dish, place the cornstarch. In another dish, beat the eggs.
  • Dip each pork piece in the beaten eggs, then coat evenly with cornstarch.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a deep skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Fry the coated pork pieces in batches until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  • In a separate pan, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and saut√© until fragrant.
  • Add the diced onion and bell pepper to the pan, and cook until they start to soften.
  • Pour in the ketchup, rice vinegar, brown sugar, and soy sauce. Stir well to combine.
  • Add the pineapple chunks to the sauce and bring to a simmer.
  • In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch with water to make a slurry. Pour the slurry into the sauce, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
  • Add the fried pork pieces to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  • Serve the sweet and sour pork hot, garnished with sliced green onions if desired.
  • Enjoy your homemade Sweet and Sour Pork!
