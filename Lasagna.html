Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned and onion is softened.
  • Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, and oregano; simmer for 10 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, mix together mozzarella cheese, ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  • In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer 1/3 of the noodles, 1/2 of the meat sauce, and 1/2 of the cheese mixture.
  • Repeat with another layer of noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture.
  • Top with a final layer of noodles and the remaining meat sauce.
  • Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
