Homemade Potato Chips

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Salt, to taste
  • Optional seasonings: paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, etc.

Instructions

  • Scrub the potatoes clean under running water and pat them dry with a kitchen towel.
  • Using a mandoline slicer or a sharp knife, slice the potatoes thinly (about 1/16 inch thick).
  • Place the potato slices in a bowl of cold water and let them soak for about 30 minutes. This helps remove excess starch and makes the chips crispier.
  • After soaking, drain the potato slices and pat them dry with a clean kitchen towel or paper towels.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  • Fry the potato slices in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the fryer. Fry for about 3-4 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the chips from the oil using a slotted spoon and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  • Season the chips with salt and any other desired seasonings while they are still hot.
  • Let the chips cool completely before serving. Store any leftovers in an airtight container.
  • Enjoy your crispy and flavorful homemade potato chips!
