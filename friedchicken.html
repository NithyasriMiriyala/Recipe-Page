Korean Fried Chicken (KFC)

Ingredients for Chicken

  • 2 lbs chicken wings or drumsticks
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Ingredients for Sauce

  • 1/4 cup gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  • In a bowl, mix together all-purpose flour, cornstarch, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
  • Coat the chicken pieces with the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  • Fry the chicken in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes for wings and 12-15 minutes for drumsticks. Drain on paper towels.
  • In a separate bowl, mix together gochujang, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced garlic, and sesame oil to make the sauce.
  • Toss the fried chicken in the sauce until evenly coated.
  • Garnish with toasted sesame seeds and sliced green onions.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Korean Fried Chicken!
