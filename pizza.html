Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, etc.)

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  • Spread tomato sauce evenly over dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over sauce.
  • Add toppings of your choice.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted and bubbly.
