Chinese Mooncakes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup golden syrup
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon lye water
  • 1/2 cup lotus seed paste
  • 4 salted egg yolks
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, golden syrup, vegetable oil, and lye water until a smooth dough forms.
  2. Cover thedough with plastic wrap and let it rest for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  4. Divide the dough into 4 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a ball.
  5. Divide the lotus seed paste into 4 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a ball.
  6. Flatten each ball of dough into a circle. Place a ball of lotus seed paste and a salted egg yolk in the center of each circle.
  7. Wrap the dough around the filling, sealing it completely. Roll the ball in cornstarch to prevent sticking.
  8. Place the balls on the prepared baking sheet. Brush the tops with beaten egg.
  9. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the tops are golden brown.
  10. Let the mooncakes cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
