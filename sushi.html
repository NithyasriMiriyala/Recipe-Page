Homemade Sushi Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 4 nori seaweed sheets
  • Assorted fillings (e.g., cucumber sticks, avocado slices, cooked shrimp, smoked salmon, crab sticks)
  • Soy sauce, for serving
  • Pickled ginger, for serving
  • Wasabi, for serving

Instructions

  • Cook sushi rice according to package instructions. Let it cool slightly.
  • Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
  • Spread a thin layer of sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving about 1 inch of space at the top edge.
  • Arrange your desired fillings in the center of the rice.
  • Using the bamboo mat or towel, tightly roll the sushi away from you, starting from the edge closest to you.
  • Once rolled, seal the edge of the nori by moistening it slightly with water.
  • Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into 6-8 pieces.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining nori sheets and fillings.
  • Serve the sushi rolls with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi on the side.
