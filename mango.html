Mango Sticky Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice (sticky rice)
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Toasted sesame seeds or mung beans (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  • Rinse the sticky rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Then, soak the rice in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  • Drain the soaked rice and steam it in a bamboo steamer or a steaming basket lined with cheesecloth or a clean kitchen towel, for about 25-30 minutes until tender and cooked through.
  • While the rice is steaming, prepare the coconut sauce. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, granulated sugar, and salt. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar is completely dissolved. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  • Transfer the cooked sticky rice to a large bowl. Pour half of the coconut sauce over the rice and gently mix until the rice is evenly coated with the sauce.
  • To serve, place a portion of the sweetened sticky rice on a plate or in a bowl. Arrange slices of ripe mango on top or alongside the rice.
  • Drizzle the remaining coconut sauce over the mango and rice.
  • Optionally, garnish with toasted sesame seeds or mung beans for added texture and flavor.
  • Serve the mango sticky rice warm or at room temperature, and enjoy this delightful Thai dessert!
