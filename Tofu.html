Japanese Tofu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Drain the tofuand press it to remove excess water. Cut it into 1-inch cubes.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake, water, sugar, ginger, and garlic. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and cook until it is golden brown on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour the soy sauce mixture over the tofu and simmer for 5 minutes, or until the tofu has absorbed most of the liquid.
  5. Transfer the tofu to a serving dish and sprinkle with green onions and sesame seeds.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice and vegetables, if desired.
