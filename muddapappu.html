Muddapappu Annam

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2-3 dry red chilies
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves (cilantro)

Instructions

  • Wash rice and toor dal together and soak them in water for about 15-20 minutes.
  • In a pressure cooker, add the soaked rice and dal mixture along with 3 cups of water and salt. Cook until soft and mushy.
  • In a separate pan, heat ghee or oil. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add cumin seeds, dry red chilies, asafoetida, curry leaves, and slit green chilies. Saut√© for a minute.
  • Pour this tempering over the cooked rice and dal mixture. Mix well.
  • Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with a dollop of ghee and your favorite pickle.
